There are 28 member countries which are currently in the European Union and the entire continent is home to 731,000,000 people within 3,930,000 square miles. Each country has it’s own unique history and culture that is explored by many.

Personally, I have only visited part of the United Kingdom which is home to England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. In 2005, my family traveled to England and Scotland to visit many of our relatives and to explore the countries.

England is known for the royal family, castles and its rich history. Some of the biggest tourist destinations in England are ____. One “must-do” while there is taking the underground (subway system) to all the historic sites in London.

Scotland is known for its bag pipes, whiskey, kilts and beautiful lakes and scenery. Some of the biggest tourist destinations in Scotland are ____. One “must-do” while there is seeing the Edinburgh castle and walking they Royal Mile.
