There are thousands of islands that are a part of the Caribbean Sea. Some of the larger and more popular countries that have some of these islands include Aruba, British Virgin Islands, Cuba, Haiti, and Jamaica. The list can go on forever!

These islands are known for their beaches and friendly people. Some of the biggest tourist destinations within these islands include their beaches, local culture and cuisine. One “must-do” while on any island is take advantage of their beautiful beaches.

My family has been able to travel to several of these islands including Dominican Republic, the Bahamas, the US Virgin Islands, St. Martin/St. Marten, and Bermuda

Come explore!
